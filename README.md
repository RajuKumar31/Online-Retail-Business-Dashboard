# 📊 Online Retail – Revenue & Customer Insights Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes online retail transaction data using Power BI to uncover actionable insights into revenue trends, customer behavior, and global demand patterns. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by highlighting key business metrics and performance drivers.

## 📦 Demand & Quantity Insights

- Netherlands leads in both **revenue (~$0.29M)** and **quantity (~200K+ units)**
- EIRE and Germany follow with **140K+ and 120K+ units respectively**

### 🔍 Insight:
- High revenue + high quantity = **strong product-market fit**

---

## 🌐 Global Demand Insights

- Demand clusters concentrated in:
  - **Western Europe (Netherlands, Germany, France)**
  - **Australia**

### 🔍 Insight:
- Business has **global scalability potential**
- Europe is the **primary revenue-driving region outside the UK**

---

## 📊 Business Insights Summary

- 📈 Revenue is **seasonal**, peaking in November ($1.51M)
- 👑 A small group of customers drives a large share of revenue
- 🌍 Strong international demand led by European markets
- 📦 High-performing regions show both **high sales volume and revenue**
- 📉 Mid-year performance is relatively stable with growth accelerating in Q4
